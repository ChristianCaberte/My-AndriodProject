About the Game

[Bounce Your Balls] is an exciting Android game developed as a project for the Mobile App Development course. It challenges players to control the ball until it became fast and becarefully not to fall it down with unique gameplay mechanics, and an immersive experience.

Features

  • Innovative gameplay mechanics
  • Bounce The Ball and Control it
  • Beautiful graphics and animations
  • Engaging sound effects and music
